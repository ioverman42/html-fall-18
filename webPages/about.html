Shelf Indulgence is a used book store in Madison, WI that strives to spread a love of books and reading to as many people as possible. New books come in all the time so be sure to stop by the shop and check out what's new.

We also have many events for people of all ages to enjoy. Check out our Events page to learn more.

Consider becoming a friend of Shelf Indulgence to receive our newsletter and find out about all the latest happenings here before anyone else! Visit the Become a Friend page to learn more and become one of our valued friends.

We feature a book of the month and an author of the month on this website, so check out this month's and come back next month for more!
